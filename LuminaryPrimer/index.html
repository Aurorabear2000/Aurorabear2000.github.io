    What is LuminaryPrimer

    LuminaryPrimer is a tool software focused on providing reference of light and shadow relationship. It aims to simplify the creation process of light and shadow effects, so that users can achieve the desired light and shadow effects through presets and simple parameter adjustment without deep professional skills.
