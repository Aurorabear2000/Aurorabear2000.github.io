For the crowd

AuroraBearDecember 11, 2024Less than 1 minute
On This Page
  • Painting artists
  • Photography videographer
  • Animation producer
  • Indie game developers

Painting artists

LuminaryPrimer provides an easy and intuitive way to explore and experiment with different light and shadow effects to help your work come alive and have depth. With preset lighting effects, you can quickly find the right lighting configuration for your style and refine the effect with simple parameter adjustments.

Photography videographer

LuminaryPrimer can be used to simulate the effects of various lighting conditions to preview the final photo or video before you take it. This is very useful for planning the shooting process and choosing the best time and Angle to shoot.

Animation producer

LuminaryPrimer can help them control light and shadow changes more precisely during the creation process, enhancing the realism and visual impact of the animation. Preset effects in the software serve as a starting point for inspiration, while detailed parameter tuning allows you to customize unique lighting effects to your needs.

Indie game developers

LuminaryPrimer can be used to enhance the visual effects of a game and create a more engaging game world.
